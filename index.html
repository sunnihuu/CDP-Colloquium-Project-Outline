Initial Idea

How Does Food Travel and Transform?

"The delicious rice noodles we are familiar with today, the beef pho with mint, cilantro,
Thai basil, lime, and chili, each step in its developmental history is intertwined with
the heartbreaking journey of Vietnamese refugees escaping from
North to South Vietnam, and then from East to West.” -- Yu Feng

NYC residents are facing rising household food costs.

About 3 million New Yorkers reside in neighborhoods classified as food deserts, lacking convenient access to fresh food.

The NYC food system also faces challenges such as supply gaps, food insecurity, and a growing meal gap.

This is not just a problem in New York City, it's a global issue.
