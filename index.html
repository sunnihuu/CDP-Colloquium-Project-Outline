How Does Food Travel and Transform?

"The delicious rice noodles we are familiar with today, the beef pho with mint, cilantro,
Thai basil, lime, and chili, each step in its developmental history is intertwined with
the heartbreaking journey of Vietnamese refugees escaping from
North to South Vietnam, and then from East to West.” -- Yu Feng

Rising Household Food Costs

From 2012 to 2023, the cost of food at home increased by 65.8%, rising faster than dining out.

Source: New York State Office of the State Comptroller, 2025

Low-Income Families Struggle

In 2022–2023, families earning under $15K per year spent nearly 70% of their income on food.s

Source: New York State Office of the State Comptroller, 2025

Food Deserts in NYC

About 3 million NYC residents live in neighborhoods with poor access to grocery stores.

Source: New York State Office of the State Comptroller, 2025
